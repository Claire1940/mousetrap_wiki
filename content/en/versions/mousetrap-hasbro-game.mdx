| Issue | Common Cause | Corrective Action |
|---|---|---|

<Section icon={Shield} title="How to Trigger and Reset the Trap">

Triggering the trap initiates a complex physical sequence. Understanding the mechanics allows players to run the game smoothly and identify mechanical failures quickly.

**The Chain Reaction Sequence:**
1. The player pulls the stop sign back, releasing the spring-loaded boot.
2. The boot swings forward and kicks the bucket at the bottom of the stairs.
3. The kick tips the bucket, sending the first marble rolling down the stairs.
4. The marble enters the rain gutter and drops into the second bucket.
5. The weight of the marble pulls the broomstick down, releasing the wrench latch.
6. The broom swings, hitting the diving board.
7. The diver is launched off the board and lands in the wash tub.
8. The impact of the diver in the tub shakes the pole, sending the cage sliding down to capture the mouse.
