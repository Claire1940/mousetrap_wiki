<Section icon={Shield} title="Mastering the Trap Mechanics">

The mechanical contraption is the heart of the game. However, it is also a point of failure. If the trap fails to trigger or the cage doesn't fall correctly, you gain no cheese. Mastering the physical side of the game is just as important as the strategy.

When you land on the **Blue "Launch the Trap" space**, you follow these sub-steps:
1.  Choose one opponent.
2.  Move their token to the designated Trap Space.
3.  Pull back the stop sign and release.
4.  Observe the Rube Goldberg sequence: the ball rolls, the diver jumps, and the cage falls.

If the cage successfully captures the mouse, you take one cheese slice from that player. If it fails—even if the ball just gets stuck—the player is safe, and you get nothing.

| Component | Common Issue | Solution |
|-----------|--------------|----------|
| **Ball Diver** | Doesn't trigger ball | Ensure the diver is fully clicked back |
| **Broom** | Gets stuck | Rotate the broom up before every launch |
| **Cage** | Falls off track | Check the alignment of the vertical post |
| **Stop Sign** | Too much friction | Pull and release sharply, don't "guide" it |

<Callout type="warning" title="Trap Maintenance">
Always reset the trap components immediately after a launch. If you forget to return the ball or reset the cage, you might lose your turn's advantage when you land on a Blue space again.
</Callout>
