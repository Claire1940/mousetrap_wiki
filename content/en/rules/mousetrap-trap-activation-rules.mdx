Movement proceeds clockwise around the board based on the roll of a single die. Players must move their token the exact number of spaces rolled. Landing on different spaces triggers specific actions, ranging from collecting cheese to initiating the trap sequence.

| Space Color | Space Name | Action Required |
|-|-|-|
| Blue | Launch the Trap | Move opponent to trap and trigger contraption |
| Yellow | Cheese Space | Collect one cheese slice from the main pile |
| Green | Steal Space | Take one cheese slice from any opponent |
| Red | Lose a Cheese | Return one cheese slice to the supply pile |
| Purple | Safe Space | No action is required on this space |

<Section icon={Target} title="Mousetrap Trap Activation Rules">

The core mechanic of the game revolves around the mousetrap trap activation rules. When a player lands on the blue "Launch the Trap" space, they gain the opportunity to capture an opponent's mouse token. This process requires relocating the target mouse to the designated trap space before triggering the physical contraption. If the trap successfully lands over the target mouse, the capturing player claims a cheese slice from the victim.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Target Selection">
    Identify an opponent and move their mouse token directly onto the red trap space on the board.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Trigger the Contraption">
    Pull back on the stop sign component and release it to start the chain reaction.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Observe the Trap Resolution">
    Watch the cage descend. If the cage successfully traps the target mouse, the activation is successful.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Collect the Reward">
    Claim one cheese slice from the trapped player. If multiple mice are on the space, claim cheese from all of them.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If the trap fails to capture the target mouse for any mechanical reason, the turn ends immediately, and no cheese is exchanged. The player who initiated the trap must then reset the mechanism for the next turn.

| Scenario | Target Location | Success Condition | Reward |
|-|-|-|-|
| Single Target | Trap Space | Cage lands fully over the mouse | 1 Cheese from target |
| Multiple Targets | Trap Space | Cage lands over all mice | 1 Cheese from each |
| Mechanical Failure | Trap Space | Cage fails to drop or align | 0 Cheese exchanged |
| Empty Trap Space | Safe Space | Cannot trigger activation sequence | No action taken |

<Callout type="warning" title="Trap Failures">
If the cage does not fall completely due to a setup misalignment, the trap is ruled a failure. The target player does not surrender any cheese, and the active player must reset the board.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Resetting the Contraption & Tunnel Mechanics">

After every trap activation attempt, whether successful or not, the trap must be reset. This ensures the board remains ready for the next player who lands on a launch space. Resetting involves returning the ball, the diver, the cage, and the broom to their original starting positions.

Additionally, players can navigate the board efficiently by using the mouse hole tunnel space. Landing on this space allows a player to travel through the tunnel to the space on the opposite end, offering a tactical shortcut to avoid hazards or reach cheese spaces faster.

| Component | Reset Action | Correct State |
|-|-|-|
| Ball | Place back in the top holder | Secured in place |
| Diver | Return to the top platform | Standing upright |
| Cage | Lift to the top of the post | Suspended high |
| Broom | Rotate back to the upright position | Ready to swing |
