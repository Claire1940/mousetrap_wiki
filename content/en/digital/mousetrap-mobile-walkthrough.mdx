<Section icon={Shield} title="Digital Board Game Rules & Turn Mechanics">

The digital version of Mouse Trap simplifies the traditional tabletop experience by automating the complex assembly of the Rube Goldberg trap. The primary objective is to collect, steal, and protect cheese. The game concludes immediately when the Cheese Machine runs out of its supply, and the player with the most cheese in their inventory wins.

Each turn consists of spinning the wheel, moving your mouse, and resolving the land space's prompt. The board features several specialized spaces that can completely shift the momentum of the game.

| Space Type | Board Visual | Turn Action Required | Cheese Impact |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Cheese Space | Yellow Wedge | Collect cheese immediately | +1 to +3 Cheese |
| Steal Space | Red Paw Print | Choose a rival to steal from | +2 Cheese (Target -2) |
| Trap Space | Red Mechanical | Trigger the action contraption | Disrupts target's turn |
| Event Space | Animated Gear | Participate in mini-games | High risk, high reward |

<Callout type="warning" title="Gameplay Warning">
Do not rely on physical board game rules. The digital edition features unique scoring mechanics and does not eliminate players when they are caught in the trap; instead, it penalizes their cheese totals.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="The Action Contraption & Chain Reaction Guide">

The famous action contraption is the centerpiece of Mouse Trap. In the digital version, the trap is fully animated and can be triggered when a player lands on a designated trap space. Activating the trap initiates a multi-stage Rube Goldberg sequence that ends with a cage dropping onto an exposed mouse.

Understanding the stages of this sequence helps you anticipate the timing and plan your movements around the board to avoid being caught.

| Sequence Stage | Triggering Component | Animation Detail | Gameplay Result |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Stage 1 | The Boot | Swings and kicks the bucket | Sequence initiates |
| Stage 2 | The Ball | Rolls down the winding chute | Suspense phase |
| Stage 3 | The Diver | Launches from the springboard | Final mechanical trigger |
| Stage 4 | The Cage | Drops onto the targeted space | Target loses turn/cheese |
