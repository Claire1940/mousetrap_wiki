<Section icon={Shield} title="Survival Tactics and Obstacle Avoidance">

Survival in the **mousetrap game online** depends entirely on your ability to manage the "Bleed" mechanic. When the mouse touches a wall, it begins to bleed, and the health bar at the bottom of the screen depletes. Once the bar reaches zero, the run ends immediately.

To survive the more difficult stages, players must master the following tactics:

1.  **The Center-Path Rule**: Always attempt to keep the mouse in the exact center of any corridor. This provides the maximum margin for error if your hand slips.
2.  **Momentum Braking**: If the mouse is moving too fast toward a wall, move your cursor back toward the mouse to slow it down.
3.  **Obstacle Timing**: Moving obstacles like axes follow a set rhythmic pattern. Observe the swing for two cycles before attempting to pass.

| Obstacle Type | Danger Level | Survival Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Static Walls** | Moderate | Maintain center alignment |
| **Sharp Corners** | High | Slow down cursor speed before turn |
| **Swinging Axes** | Extreme | Wait for the full swing cycle |
| **Narrow Gaps** | High | Use micro-movements with the mouse |

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
Do not get greedy for food items located near moving axes. The point bonus is rarely worth the risk of a game-ending health loss, especially in deeper levels.
</Callout>

Navigating the complex stages of the **mousetrap game online** requires a methodical approach. Follow these steps to improve your consistency and reach the higher levels.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Environment Assessment">
    Before moving the mouse, scan the entire level. Identify where the cheese is located and look for the safest path that avoids unnecessary tight corners.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear Initial Food">
    Collect the food items in the wider areas first. This builds your score early and allows you to get a feel for the mouse's response time in that specific session.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Navigate Choke Points">
    When approaching a narrow corridor, reduce your cursor movement speed. Use small, incremental "taps" of the mouse cursor to lead the character through the gap.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Timing the Hazards">
    For levels with axes or moving traps, stop the mouse in a safe zone. Count the seconds between swings and move only when the path is completely clear.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Final Approach">
    Once near the cheese, do not rush. Many players lose their final bit of health right at the end due to over-excitement. Maintain your center-path discipline until the level transition triggers.
  </Step>
</Steps>
