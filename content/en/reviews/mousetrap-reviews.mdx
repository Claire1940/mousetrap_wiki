<Section icon={Target} title="Core Rules & Gameplay Mechanics">

The digital game replaces the physical win condition of being the last surviving mouse with a high-stakes, point-based race. To win a match, you must collect, steal, and protect cheese before the central Cheese Machine runs dry. This structural shift keeps all four players active until the very end of the game, preventing early player elimination.

The turn flow is straightforward, making it highly accessible for younger players while retaining tactical depth for adults.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Mode & Customization">
    Choose your play style (Solo, Local, or Online) and customize your mouse with unlocked outfits and classic colors.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Roll & Move">
    Take turns rolling the digital die to navigate the vibrant, circular board. Landing on spaces triggers immediate prompts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Interact & Collect">
    Gather cheese from board spaces, trigger the Riggs the Cat interactive mini-game, or steal direct from your opponents' piles.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Trigger the Trap">
    Land on designated spaces to initiate the Rube Goldberg contraption, dropping the cage to disrupt rival mice and swing the score.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Gameplay Difference">
Unlike the traditional physical board game, you do not build the trap piece-by-piece. The digital machine is fully assembled from the start, allowing the chain reaction to trigger dynamically throughout the match.
</Callout>
