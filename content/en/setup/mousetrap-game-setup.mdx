<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Board Assembly Guide">

Follow these sequence instructions to construct the trap mechanism. The board features designated slots and numbers that correspond to each base. Assembling the parts out of order can lead to alignment issues, so it is best to follow the steps sequentially.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure Base One and the Lamppost">
    Take Base 1 and insert it into the slots on the board. Slide it to lock it into place, ensuring all tabs are fully inserted. Insert the stop sign and the lamppost into their designated slots on Base 1. Press down firmly on the lamppost until it snaps into place. Attach the rubber band to Base 1 and the stop sign, then attach the boot to the lamppost. Ensure the heel of the boot rests against the stop sign.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Build the Stairs and Rain Gutter">
    Insert stair legs A into the board and push them back to lock them. Attach stair legs B to the back of the stairs. Connect the back legs of the assembled stairs to Base 1 while attaching the front of the stairs to legs A. Attach bucket 1 to the top of the stairs and place a marble inside. Secure Base 3 into the board, slide it to lock, and then connect Base 2 to Bases 1 and 3. Attach the rain gutter to the bottom of the stairs and bases.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Mount the Ladder and Broom">
    Push the plastic wrench onto the ladder, then insert the ladder into Base 2. Attach bucket 2 to the broom handle, and mount the broom onto the ladder. Rotate the broom so that the bucket is positioned near the end of the rain gutter. Push the bucket in slightly so it clears the gutter, then turn the wrench upward to latch the broomstick in place. This holds the broom in its primed position.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Assemble the Plumbing, Tub, and Cage">
    Insert the plumbing pipes into Base 3, then attach the bathtub to the plumbing unit. Connect the short ramp to the bathtub and the plumbing. Attach the diving board to Base 3, and place the diver figure on the end of the board. Secure Base 4 to the board and place the wash tub on it. Insert the cage pole into its slot, and balance the cage on top of the pole.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Tension Check">

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Gameplay Rules and Trap Mechanics">

Once the **mousetrap game setup** is complete, players take turns rolling the die and moving their mouse tokens around the board. The primary goal is to collect cheese slices and avoid getting trapped by your opponents. Players can acquire cheese by landing on designated cheese spaces or by successfully trapping an opponent's mouse.

When a player lands on the "Turn Crank" space and another player is on the cheese wheel space, the active player can attempt to trigger the trap. This is done by turning the crank, which rotates the gear, kicks the lever, and starts the chain reaction.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Troubleshooting and Maintenance">

Over time, plastic parts can become misaligned, causing the trap to fail during gameplay. Regular maintenance and careful handling will ensure the mechanism operates smoothly. If the marble does not trigger the broom, or if the cage fails to fall, refer to the troubleshooting steps below.

| Issue | Likely Cause | Recommended Solution |
|--- |--- |--- |
| Boot fails to swing | Rubber band is loose or degraded | Replace or tighten the rubber band |
| Broom does not release | Wrench is latched too tightly | Adjust wrench position slightly |
| Cage does not fall | Pole is misaligned or unbalanced | Re-center the cage on the support peg |
