<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Mousetrap Chain Reaction Setup">

Executing a perfect **mousetrap chain reaction setup** requires observing the board's flow and understanding the six distinct stages of the digital contraption. The animation is designed to build suspense, as the sequence can occasionally fail before reaching the final cage drop.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Watch the Trap Area">
    Monitor the board as rivals approach the designated Trap Zone. This area often contains high-value cheese rewards, acting as bait for greedy players. Track the current cheese totals of any mouse entering this quadrant.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Choose the Strategic Moment">
    When the prompt appears, evaluate if trapping the exposed mouse will meaningfully change the standings. In 2026 competitive play, the best time to trigger the setup is when the leader has a narrow margin or the Cheese Machine is nearly empty.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate the Boot Swing">
    The sequence begins with the mechanical boot swinging to kick the marble. This starts the physical momentum of the Rube Goldberg machine. Once this begins, the animation must resolve before the next player's turn.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Track the Marble Flow">
    The marble travels through a series of chutes and obstacles, eventually landing in the bathtub. This stage determines the initial success of the chain reaction; if the marble misses, the trap fails early.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Launch the Diver">
    The weight of the marble triggers the springboard, launching the diver into the tub. This is the penultimate stage of the setup. During this animation, players should plan their next collection move based on the likely outcome.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="The Final Cage Drop">
    The cage falls over the targeted mouse. If successful, the captured player is disrupted, allowing you to move toward valuable cheese piles while they recover. A successful capture often results in a significant score swing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Success Strategy">
A successful trap resolution is the best time to pivot from defensive play to aggressive cheese collection. Use the captured player's downtime to secure the final pieces from the Cheese Machine.
</Callout>

</Section>
