<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Gutter and Ladder Sequence">

Once the foundations are set, the next phase of the **mousetrap pieces setup** involves the vertical elements. This is where the kinetic energy is transferred from the initial trigger to the middle of the board. The stairs and the rain gutter must be perfectly aligned so the marble does not fall off the track.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Stair Assembly">
    Insert stair legs A into the board and lock them. Attach stair legs B to the back of the stairs, then connect the entire unit to Base 1.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Bucket and Marble Placement">
    Attach Bucket 1 to the top of the stairs. Place the marble inside. This is the weight that will eventually roll down the gutter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Gutter Installation">
    Connect the rain gutter to the bottom of the stairs and anchor it to the pegs on Bases 1 and 2. Press down firmly to ensure a smooth transition for the marble.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Ladder and Broom Setup">
    Insert the ladder into Base 2. Attach Bucket 2 to the broom, then latch the broom to the ladder using the wrench. This holds the secondary trigger in place.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Component | Connection Point | Interaction |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stairs** | Base 1 / Legs A | Supports Bucket 1 and the rolling marble |
| **Rain Gutter** | Stairs / Base 2 | Directs marble into Bucket 2 |
| **Wrench** | Ladder / Broom | Holds the broom until the marble hits Bucket 2 |

<Callout type="info" title="Physics Note">
The wrench must be turned upward to latch the broom. If it is too loose, the broom will fall before the marble arrives. If too tight, the marble's weight won't be enough to trigger the release.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Final Trap and Cage Placement">

The final stage of the setup focuses on the bathtub, the diver, and the cage. This is the most delicate part of the assembly, as the cage must balance perfectly on the pole to ensure it drops vertically over the "Trap" space.

1. **Plumbing and Bathtub**: Insert the plumbing into Base 3 and attach the bathtub to the top.
2. **Short Ramp**: Connect the short ramp between the bathtub and the plumbing exit.
3. **Diving Board and Diver**: Attach the diving board to Base 3 and place the diver on the very edge.
4. **Wash Tub and Cage**: Place the wash tub on Base 4. Insert the cage pole and balance the cage on the small notch at the top.

| Part | Role in the Trap | Critical Check |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Bathtub** | Receives the diver | Must be level to ensure the diver bounces correctly |
| **Diving Board** | Launches the diver | Check for flexibility and secure base attachment |
| **Cage Pole** | Holds the cage | Must be perfectly vertical for a clean drop |

<Section icon={Swords} title="Testing and Troubleshooting">

Even with a careful **mousetrap pieces setup**, mechanical failures can occur. Most issues are related to friction or improper part alignment. Before starting a competitive game, perform 2-3 test runs to ensure the marble rolls smoothly and the cage drops every time.

| :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Broom won't release** | Wrench is jammed | Lubricate the plastic joint or adjust latch tension |
| **Cage misses the tub** | Pole is leaning | Re-seat Base 4 and ensure the pole is straight |
| **Diver misses bathtub** | Diving board loose | Re-attach diving board and check diver balance |

<Callout type="warning" title="Resetting the Trap">
After every successful capture, you must manually reset the trap. This involves moving the marble back to the stairs, re-latching the broom, and re-balancing the cage.
</Callout>
