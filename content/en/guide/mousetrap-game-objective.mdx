<Section icon={Swords} title="The Rube Goldberg Trap Chain Reaction">

The highlight of the game is the activation of the mechanical trap. This complex sequence of physical actions must work flawlessly to capture an opposing mouse and secure your progress toward the **mousetrap game objective**.

The trap is triggered by turning the crank, which initiates a chain reaction across several interconnected parts.

| Step | Action | Mechanical Trigger |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Turn the Crank | Rotates the gear mechanism |
| **2** | Kick the Bucket | The boot strikes the bucket, releasing the marble |
| **3** | Roll the Marble | The marble rolls down the steps into the gutter |
| **4** | Tilt the Giraffe | The second marble strikes the giraffe, causing it to tilt |
| **5** | Drop the Cage | The monkey twists down, releasing the cage onto the cheese trap |

If the sequence completes successfully, the cage drops over the cheese trap space. Any mouse currently positioned on that space is captured and eliminated from the game.

<Callout type="tip" title="Troubleshooting Tip">
If the trap fails to trigger due to a mechanical error, the target player is not eliminated. Reset the trap and continue gameplay.
</Callout>
