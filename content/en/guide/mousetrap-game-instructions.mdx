<Section icon={Target} title="Navigating the Board Spaces">

Understanding the **mousetrap game instructions** for board spaces is vital for developing a winning strategy. Each color-coded space triggers a specific action that can either advance your progress or hinder your opponents. Movement is strictly clockwise, and players must move the exact number of spaces shown on the die.

| Space Color | Name | Action Required |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Yellow** | Cheese Space | Collect **one cheese slice** from the pile |
| **Green** | Steal Space | Take **one cheese slice** from any opponent |
| **Red** | Lose Cheese | Return **one cheese slice** to the pile |
| **Blue** | Launch Trap | Attempt to capture an opponent in the trap |
| **Purple** | Neutral | No action occurs on this space |
| **Brown** | Mouse Hole | Optional travel through the tunnel to the exit |

The Mouse Hole tunnel is a strategic element often overlooked by beginners. When landing on a tunnel entrance, you may choose to immediately move your token to the tunnel's exit on the opposite side of the board. This can be used to bypass "Lose a Cheese" spaces or to position yourself closer to the "Launch the Trap" zones.

<Callout type="tip" title="Strategic Positioning">
If you land on a Green "Steal" space, always target the player currently leading with the most cheese slices to prevent them from completing their circle.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="How to Trigger the Trap">

The heart of the game is the trap mechanism. Triggering the trap is the primary way to set back your opponents while potentially gaining cheese for yourself. According to the **mousetrap game instructions**, the trap can only be activated when a player lands on a blue "Launch the Trap" space.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify the Target">
    When you land on a blue space, choose one opponent to move to the "Trap" space on the board.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Initiate the Sequence">
    Pull back on the stop sign trigger and release it. This starts the mechanical chain reaction.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Observe the Outcome">
    Watch as the marble rolls, the diver jumps, and the cage falls. If the cage successfully lands over the opponent's mouse, the trap is successful.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Collect the Reward">
    A trapped player must give you **one slice of cheese**. If multiple mice were on the trap space, you collect cheese from all of them.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Reset the Trap">
    Return the ball, diver, and cage to their original positions and rotate the broom back up for the next turn.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If the trap fails for any reason—such as a piece getting stuck or the cage falling off-center—the target player remains safe and does not lose any cheese. This mechanical unpredictability is a core feature of the Mousetrap experience.

<Callout type="success" title="Multi-Mouse Capture">
It is possible for more than one mouse to be on the trap space at the same time. If the trap succeeds, the player who triggered it receives cheese from every single mouse caught under the cage.
</Callout>

</Section>

Managing your cheese is just as important as collecting it. Since you can lose cheese on Red spaces or have it stolen on Green spaces, maintaining a lead requires careful movement.

| Strategy Phase | Priority | Key Action |
